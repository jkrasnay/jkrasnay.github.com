About Me

I'm a professional Java developer with over 16 years of development, system architecture, and team lead experience. I'm available for contract work in the Greater Toronto Area. See my resume for more details.

My major open source contribution is the Vex XML editor. Vex has been adopted by the Eclipse WTP Incubator and will hopefully one day be the official visual XML editor for Eclipse. I was recently interviewed about Vex for an article in the German computer magazine Eclipse Magazin.
